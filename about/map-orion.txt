-  1. Setting                                                                  -
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   The Orion I is a human support ship of the type Orion.
   Its main function is to coordinate the communication of the human ships.
   Recently, an asteroid crushed into the maintenance sector of the Orion I.
   The sensors are noticed alien activity after the impact.
   Sector B has been evacuated.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
-  2. About                                                                    -
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   I wanted to create a custom Tremulous map with an own small story.
   The map is divided into two parts:
    - the processing sector (human side)
    - the mainenance sector (alien side)

   Both sectors differ from the layout, lighting and the average felling of the
   rooms in this sector.
